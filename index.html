Family Planning Calculator

Welcome to Your Family Financial Planner

This calculator, a collaboration between FinMango and Modern Husbands, helps dual-income families evaluate their financial trajectory over the next 15 years. Factors include temporarily leaving the workforce, household income, childcare costs for one child, healthcare expenses, taxes, and retirement savings.

We've pre-populated the fields with example numbers. Feel free to adjust these to match your specific situation.
