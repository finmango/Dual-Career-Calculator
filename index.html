Dual Career Calculator

Welcome to Your Family Financial Planner

This calculator, a collaboration between FinMango and Modern Husbands, helps dual-income families evaluate their financial trajectory over the next 15 years. It focuses on the long-term financial implications of temporarily leaving the workforce for childcare versus using daycare/childcare services.

We've pre-populated the fields with example numbers. Feel free to adjust these to match your specific situation.
